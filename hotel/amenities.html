Facilities & Amenities

From our restaurant to our event hall — every facility at HOTEL NAME is designed to make your stay exceptional.

Restaurant & Bar

Our in-house restaurant serves Nigerian and continental dishes from 6:30am to 10pm daily. Full breakfast, à la carte lunch, and dinner menu. Bar open until midnight.

Swimming Pool

Outdoor pool with sun loungers and poolside service. Open 7am–8pm daily. Complimentary for all hotel guests. Towels provided at the pool desk.

Fitness Centre

Fully equipped gym with cardio machines, free weights, and resistance equipment. Open 5am–10pm. Complimentary for hotel guests.

Conference & Event Hall

Fully air-conditioned event space for meetings, seminars, weddings, and celebrations. Seats up to 200 guests. AV equipment, microphones, and projector included.

Spa & Wellness

Relax and unwind with massage therapy, facial treatments, and body scrubs. Book a session at reception or via WhatsApp. Open daily 9am–8pm.

🅿 Secured Parking

Spacious gated parking compound with 24/7 security coverage. Free for all guests. CCTV monitored. Adequate space for cars, SUVs, and buses.

More Services

Additional Services

24/7 Room Service

Hot meals and beverages delivered to your room around the clock. Full menu available 6am–10pm; snacks and drinks all night.

Airport Transfers

Reliable airport pickup and drop-off service to Murtala Muhammed International Airport. Pre-book via WhatsApp before arrival.

Laundry Service

Same-day laundry and dry cleaning available Monday to Saturday. Drop items at reception by 9am for same-day return by 6pm.

Free High-Speed WiFi

Fast, reliable WiFi throughout the property — in rooms, the restaurant, the lobby, and the event hall. Separate business-grade connection available.

Full Generator Backup

Uninterrupted power supply throughout the entire property. No blackouts, no heat. Your comfort is guaranteed regardless of PHCN supply.

Special Occasion Setups

Celebrating a birthday, anniversary, or honeymoon? Let us know in advance and we will arrange floral decor, candles, and a special breakfast in your room.
