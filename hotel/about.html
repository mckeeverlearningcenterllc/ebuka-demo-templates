How It Started

A Passion for Hospitality

Palms Continental Hotel was founded in 2015 with a single goal: to offer travellers in Lagos an accommodation experience that combines the warmth of Nigerian hospitality with world-class comfort and professionalism.

Located in the heart of Victoria Island, we have welcomed thousands of guests — business travellers, families, diaspora returnees, and tourists — all looking for a clean, safe, and well-run place to stay.

Today, Palms Continental Hotel stands as one of AREA's most trusted hospitality brands — recommended by guests who return year after year.
