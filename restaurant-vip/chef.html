The Story

"I grew up watching my grandmother cook. She had no recipes — just memory, instinct, and love. I have spent my career trying to bottle that feeling."

Born and raised in Victoria Island, Lagos State, Chef CHEF NAME developed an obsession with Nigerian food from childhood — the smoky fire of party jollof, the deep complexity of well-made egusi, the pure comfort of fresh puff puff.

After training at the Culinary Institute of Africa in Johannesburg and staging at several Michelin-starred kitchens in London and Paris, Chef CHEF NAME returned to Lagos with one mission: to give Nigerian cuisine the fine dining platform it has always deserved.

At Aso Dining, every dish is a collaboration between classical French technique and the vivid, unapologetic flavours of Nigerian cooking. Nothing is dumbed down. Nothing is hidden. Everything is intentional.
