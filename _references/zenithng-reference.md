# Reference: ZenithNG Sample Site

**URL:** https://zenithng-sample-site.netlify.app
**Type:** Business consulting / professional services
**Market:** Nigerian SMEs and entrepreneurs

---

## Design Notes

**Color scheme:**
- Primary dark green: `#0D3B2E`, `#1A5C44`
- Accent gold: `#C9922A`, `#F0C96A`
- Background cream: `#FAF6EE`
- Text: `#1C1C1C`

**Sections used:**
1. Hero — headline + key stats (150+ clients, 7 years, ₦2B+ revenue)
2. About — local expertise + international credentials
3. Services — 6 service cards (strategy, finance, CAC, digital, import/export, training)
4. Testimonials — 3 client quotes
5. Contact — office address, contact form, business hours
6. Footer

---

## What Works Well (reuse in templates)
- Stats bar in hero builds trust fast — good for any established business
- 6-card services grid works well for multi-service businesses
- Gold + dark green = premium Nigerian feel
- Testimonials section — always include for Nigerian clients (trust is key)
- Mentioning CAC registration is very Nigeria-specific and relevant

## Adjustments to Consider Per Template
- Color scheme → swap to match client's brand
- Stats → update or remove if client is new/startup
- Services grid → reduce to 3 cards for simpler businesses
- Add WhatsApp button (not present here — we now add by default)
- Add Paystack for e-commerce variants
