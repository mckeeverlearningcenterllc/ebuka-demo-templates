How It Works

Simple, stress-free, from idea to outfit

01

Chat With Us on WhatsApp

Tell us about your event, the style you want, and your budget. Send us reference photos if you have them — we'll advise you on the best fabric and design.

02

Choose Your Fabric

We'll share photos of available fabrics via WhatsApp. You can pick from our stock or bring your own. We'll confirm pricing once the fabric is selected.

03

Send Your Measurements

Visit us in Bodija, Ibadan for a fitting — or send your measurements via WhatsApp. We have a simple measurement guide we can send to you.

04

Pay a Deposit

We require a 50% deposit to begin work. Balance is paid on delivery. We accept bank transfer and mobile money.

05

Receive & Slay

Pick up your outfit in Ibadan or get it delivered anywhere in Nigeria via courier. Delivery typically takes 1–2 business days after completion.
