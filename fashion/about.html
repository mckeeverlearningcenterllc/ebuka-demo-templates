Who We Are

We Dress You for Every Moment

Adire Couture was founded with one simple belief — every Nigerian woman deserves to look and feel like royalty, no matter the occasion.

From our studio in Lagos, we combine bold African prints, quality fabrics, and skilled tailoring to create pieces that celebrate who you are.

Whether you're walking down the aisle, attending an owambe, or dressing for a Monday morning — we have a style for you.
