Frequently Asked Questions

How do I know a property is genuine and not a scam?

All our listings are verified. We confirm C of O, Survey Plans, and ownership documents before listing. We also accompany you for physical inspection.

Do you work with buyers in the diaspora?

Yes. We work with Nigerians in the UK, US, Canada, and Europe. All communication is done via WhatsApp/email and we handle physical inspections on your behalf.

What is your agency fee?

Our standard agency fee is 5% of the property value for sales, and 10% of annual rent for lettings. Fees are only paid after a successful transaction.

Can you help with mortgage or financing?

Yes. We partner with leading Nigerian banks and mortgage institutions and can guide you through the process of obtaining a mortgage.
