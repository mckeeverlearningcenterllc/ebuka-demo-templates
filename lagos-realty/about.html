About Us

Trusted real estate professionals serving Lagos since 2013

Our Mission

At Prestige Property Group, our mission is to connect individuals, families, and businesses with their ideal properties through transparency, integrity, and unmatched local expertise.

We believe that every Nigerian deserves access to a safe, legally sound, and value-for-money property — whether buying, renting, or investing.

Our Story

Founded in 2013 in the heart of Lagos, Nigeria, Prestige Property Group began as a small boutique agency with a big vision: to revolutionise the property market by putting client trust first.

Over the past decade, we have grown into one of Lagos's most respected real estate firms, handling hundreds of successful transactions across Lekki, Victoria Island, Ikoyi, Ajah, and beyond.

We are proud to be registered with ESVARBON (Estate Surveyors and Valuers Registration Board of Nigeria), ensuring every transaction meets professional and legal standards.
