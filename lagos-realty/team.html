Meet Our Team

Experienced, passionate, and dedicated to getting you the right property

AO

Adebayo Okafor

Chief Executive Officer

Adebayo founded Lagos Prime Realty in 2013 after 8 years working in investment banking and property development. With an MBA from the University of Lagos and ESVARBON certification, he leads the firm's strategy and ensures every client receives world-class service. He is passionate about making property ownership accessible to every Nigerian.

CN

Chisom Nwosu

Property Consultant

Chisom is our lead property consultant with over 9 years of experience matching clients to their ideal homes and investment properties. Known for her deep knowledge of the Lekki-Ajah corridor and her warm, client-focused approach, she has successfully closed over 200 transactions. She holds a BSc in Estate Management from FUTA.

YB

Yetunde Balogun

Legal & Documentation Officer

Yetunde heads our documentation and legal department, overseeing all title searches, C of O processing, Governor's Consent applications, and tenancy agreements. A qualified Solicitor and Notary Public, she brings over 11 years of property law experience and has saved countless clients from fraudulent transactions through her meticulous due diligence.

EE

Emeka Eze

Sales Agent

Emeka is our top-performing sales agent, specialising in commercial properties and high-end residential listings on the Lagos Mainland and Island. With a background in marketing and 6 years in real estate, he is known for his energy, market insight, and ability to close deals efficiently. He is fluent in Igbo, Yoruba, and English.
