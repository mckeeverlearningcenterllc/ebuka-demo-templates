Managing Partner

Chukwuemeka Adeyemi

Senior Advocate of Nigeria (SAN)
Managing Partner  ·  Called to Bar 1998

Chukwuemeka Adeyemi founded the firm in 2004 after six years at two of Nigeria's largest commercial law firms, where he led major corporate and M&A transactions. He was appointed Senior Advocate of Nigeria in 2016 in recognition of his outstanding contribution to legal practice.

He has advised on transactions totalling more than ₦50 billion across sectors including telecommunications, oil and gas, banking, and real estate. He is widely regarded as one of the leading corporate lawyers of his generation in Nigeria.

Specialisations

  • Mergers & Acquisitions
  • Capital Markets
  • Oil & Gas Law

Education

  • LLB — University of Lagos
  • BL — Nigerian Law School
  • LLM — University of London
Senior Partner

Ngozi Okonkwo

Senior Advocate of Nigeria (SAN)
Senior Partner  ·  Called to Bar 2001

Ngozi Okonkwo joined the firm in 2007 as a Partner and was elevated to Senior Partner in 2015. She was conferred with the rank of Senior Advocate of Nigeria in 2019, making her one of fewer than a hundred female SANs in the country's history.

Her practice spans real estate law and commercial litigation, and she has led some of the most significant land and property dispute cases in Lagos and Abuja over the past fifteen years. She is known for her precise, methodical approach and her ability to navigate complex title issues efficiently.

Specialisations

  • Real Estate & Property
  • Commercial Litigation
  • Conveyancing
  • Property Dispute Resolution

Education

  • LLB — Obafemi Awolowo University
  • BL — Nigerian Law School
  • LLM — University of Ibadan
Partner

Tunde Lawson

Partner, Litigation & Dispute Resolution
Partner  ·  Called to Bar 2006

Tunde Lawson heads the firm's litigation practice and has appeared as lead counsel in more than 300 matters before Nigerian courts at all levels, including the Court of Appeal and the Supreme Court. He joined the firm in 2010 and was made a Partner in 2017.

He brings a forensic attention to detail and a calm courtroom presence that has earned the respect of colleagues and judges alike. His practice covers commercial disputes, debt recovery, injunctions, and arbitration proceedings.

Specialisations

  • Commercial Litigation
  • Arbitration
  • Injunctions & Enforcement
  • Debt Recovery

Education

  • BL — Nigerian Law School
  • MCIArb — Chartered Institute of Arbitrators
