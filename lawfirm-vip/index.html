About the Firm

A tradition of excellence in Nigerian law

Founded in 2004 by Chukwuemeka Adeyemi, SAN, our chambers has grown from a boutique corporate practice into one of Lagos' most trusted full-service law firms. We serve clients ranging from multinational corporations to families navigating complex personal matters.

We are defined not just by the results we achieve, but by how we achieve them — through clear communication, careful strategy, and an unwavering commitment to our clients' best interests at every stage of their matter.

Our team of eight qualified attorneys covers six major practice areas, with a combined track record spanning more than two decades of Nigerian legal practice.

Corporate & Commercial Law

Company formation, mergers and acquisitions, shareholders' agreements, joint ventures, regulatory compliance, and commercial contracts for businesses of all sizes.

02

Real Estate & Property Law

Conveyancing, title verification, land acquisition, property disputes, lease agreements, and mortgage transactions across Lagos and Nigeria's major commercial centres.

Litigation & Dispute Resolution

Court representation, arbitration, mediation, and alternative dispute resolution across civil, commercial, and constitutional matters before Nigerian courts at all levels.

Family Law & Succession

Employment & Labour Law
