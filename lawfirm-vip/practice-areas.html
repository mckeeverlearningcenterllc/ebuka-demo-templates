Commercial

Corporate & Commercial Law

Property

Real Estate & Property Law

Property transactions in Nigeria carry significant legal complexity, from title verification and land use charge compliance to the interplay between customary and statutory land rights. Our property practice team has advised on some of the most significant real estate transactions in Lagos and Abuja over the past two decades.

Whether you are purchasing your first home, developing a commercial estate, or resolving a long-running boundary dispute, we bring the technical precision and local knowledge your matter requires.

Key Services

  • Conveyancing and title transfer
  • Title verification and due diligence
  • Land acquisition advisory
  • Lease and tenancy agreements
  • Mortgage and charge documentation
  • Property dispute resolution
  • Governor's consent applications
  • Development joint ventures

Immigration

Immigration Law

Nigeria's immigration framework is complex and frequently updated. Errors in permit applications or non-compliance with expatriate quota obligations can have serious consequences for individuals and their employers. Our immigration practice combines deep regulatory knowledge with fast, practical support for businesses and individuals navigating Nigerian immigration requirements.

We act for multinational corporations managing large expatriate workforces as well as individuals pursuing personal residency or citizenship matters.

Key Services

  • Residence permits and STR/CERPAC
  • Subject to regularisation (STR)
  • Expatriate quota applications
  • Business visas and extensions
  • Work permit applications
  • Citizenship applications
  • Immigration compliance audits
  • Deportation and regularisation

Workplace

Employment & Labour Law

The Nigerian employment landscape is governed by a complex body of statute and common law. We advise employers on structuring compliant workplaces and advise employees on their rights when those protections are breached. Our team handles everything from the drafting of employment contracts to representation in wrongful dismissal claims before the National Industrial Court.

We also advise on collective bargaining, trade union relationships, and workforce restructuring during mergers, acquisitions, and redundancy processes.

Key Services

  • Employment contracts and handbooks
  • Wrongful and unfair dismissal
  • Redundancy and severance
  • Workplace discrimination claims
  • Trade union and CBA matters
  • HR policy compliance
  • Executive compensation
  • National Industrial Court representation
