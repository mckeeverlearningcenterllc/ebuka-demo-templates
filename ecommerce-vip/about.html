Who We Are

Our Story

Chic & Co. Lagos was founded in Lagos with a simple belief: Nigerian women deserve fashion that's beautiful, quality, and actually affordable.

We started in YEAR selling from our living room. Word spread. The orders grew. Today, we deliver premium fashion to customers across Nigeria — and we've never compromised on quality.

Every piece in our collection is handpicked and quality-checked before it ever reaches your door. We only stock what we'd wear ourselves.
