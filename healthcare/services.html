Comprehensive Care

Our Services

From everyday consultations to specialist care — we've got your family covered.

General Consultation

Our experienced GPs attend to all general health concerns — fever, pain, infections, chronic conditions, and more. Available 7 days a week.

  • Adult & paediatric consultations
  • Chronic disease management (diabetes, hypertension)
  • Prescription & referrals

Laboratory & Diagnostics

In-house laboratory for fast, accurate results. Most tests are ready within the hour.

  • Blood count, malaria, typhoid, HIV tests
  • Urinalysis & stool tests
  • Blood sugar, cholesterol, kidney & liver function
  • Pregnancy tests

Maternal & Child Health

Dedicated care for mothers and children at every stage.

  • Antenatal & postnatal care
  • Safe delivery services
  • Family planning & counselling
  • Child immunisations & well-baby clinics

Dental Care

Comprehensive dental services for the whole family.

  • Routine checkups & cleaning
  • Fillings, extractions & root canals
  • Teeth whitening & cosmetic dentistry
  • Dental X-rays

Eye Care

Eye health services for all ages.

  • Vision tests & prescription glasses
  • Treatment of eye infections & conditions
  • Glaucoma screening
  • Contact lens fittings

Vaccinations

Protection for every member of your family.

  • Full childhood immunisation schedule
  • Adult booster vaccines
  • Travel vaccinations (yellow fever, meningitis, etc.)
  • Flu & pneumonia vaccines

Ultrasound & Imaging

On-site imaging for accurate diagnosis.

  • Abdominal & pelvic ultrasound
  • Obstetric scans (pregnancy)
  • X-rays

Minor Surgery & Procedures

Common surgical procedures done safely in-clinic.

  • Wound dressing & suturing
  • Abscess drainage
  • Circumcision
  • IV drips & injections
