From Nursery to WAEC

Our Programmes

A strong, structured education from age 2 to graduation — designed to build confident, capable, and successful young Nigerians.

Ages 2 – 5

Nursery & Pre-Primary

Our Nursery and Pre-Primary section is where every great journey begins. We use play-based learning to develop curiosity, creativity, and a genuine love for education in our youngest learners.

  • Crèche, Nursery 1 & 2, Kindergarten 1 & 2
  • Phonics, early numeracy, and basic literacy
  • Arts, crafts, music, and movement activities
  • Safe, colourful, child-friendly classrooms
  • Low pupil-to-teacher ratio for individual attention
  • Weekly WhatsApp progress updates to parents

Primary 1 – 6 | Ages 6 – 11

Primary School

Our Primary School programme lays a solid academic foundation. We follow the Nigerian National Curriculum while going beyond the basics to develop well-rounded, independent learners.

  • English Language, Mathematics, Basic Science & Technology
  • Social Studies, Civic Education, and Cultural & Creative Arts
  • Computer Studies from Primary 3
  • Yoruba / French language option
  • Common Entrance preparation from Primary 5
  • Termly report cards and parent-teacher meetings

JSS 1 – SSS 3 | Ages 12 – 18

Secondary School

Our Secondary School offers a rigorous, full programme from Junior Secondary to Senior Secondary, preparing students for WAEC, NECO, and JAMB with outstanding results.

Junior Secondary (JSS 1–3)

  • English, Mathematics, Basic Science, Social Studies
  • Business Studies, Agricultural Science, French
  • Computer Science and ICT

Senior Secondary (SSS 1–3) — Three Tracks

  • Science: Physics, Chemistry, Biology, Further Maths
  • Arts: Literature, History, Government, CRS/IRS
  • Commercial: Economics, Accounting, Commerce, Marketing
  • WAEC, NECO & JAMB preparation classes

Beyond the Classroom

After-School & Extra-Curricular

Sports

Football, basketball, athletics, and table tennis. Inter-school competitions and physical fitness for all students.

Cultural Activities

Drama, dance, choir, and cultural clubs. Annual prize-giving day and cultural day celebrations.

Debate & Public Speaking

Building confidence and communication skills through our debate club and inter-school speaking competitions.

Coding Club

Introducing students to programming, robotics, and digital skills to prepare them for the technology-driven future.

After-School Lessons

Extra lessons in core subjects available Monday to Friday. Especially valuable for WAEC and Common Entrance candidates.

Agriculture & Gardening

Our school farm and gardening programme connects students with nature and practical life skills.
