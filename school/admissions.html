Step by Step

How to Apply

1

Contact Us on WhatsApp

2

We will invite you for a school tour so you and your child can see our facilities and meet our staff in person.

Make the first term payment and your child's place is confirmed. We will send you the resumption date and supply list.

Admission Requirements

Nursery & Pre-Primary

  • Child's birth certificate
  • Immunisation card (Yellow card)

Quick Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there an entrance examination?

For Primary and Secondary admissions, a short assessment is conducted to help us place your child in the correct class. It is not a pass/fail test — it is just to understand your child's current level.

Do you offer sibling discounts?

Yes. Families with two or more children enrolled receive a discount on fees. Please ask our admin team for the current sibling discount rate.

Can fees be paid in instalments?

We understand the economic realities families face. In some cases, we can arrange payment in two instalments within the term. Please speak with the bursar to discuss your situation.

Do you provide school uniforms?

Yes. School uniforms are available for purchase from our school store. On enrolment, you will receive a full list of required uniform items and their prices.

Is there a school bus service?

We have a school bus that covers select routes in AREA. Please WhatsApp us to confirm if your route is covered and to ask about the bus fee.
