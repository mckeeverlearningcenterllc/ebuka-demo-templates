Membership

Granted,
Not Purchased.

Each application is reviewed by the membership committee. Three tiers, one standard: exceptional.

The Process

Membership is a
mutual commitment.

Aso Dominion does not advertise vacancies. Members come by referral, by reputation, or by direct application reviewed against our founding criteria. Sponsorship from a current Member or Patron is preferred at the higher tiers.

The committee meets quarterly. Decisions are final and communicated privately.

Membership Tiers

Three tiers.
One uncompromising standard.

Tier One

Associate

Entry into the Aso Dominion community. Full access to common areas and the founding privileges of membership.

  • Access to all common areas and lounges
  • Priority dining reservations across all five concepts
  • Wellness centre access (spa, fitness, pool)
  • 5 guest passes per month
  • Members-only events calendar
  • Reciprocal club access — 40 cities
  • Art collection & cultural programme access
  • Dedicated concierge line
Apply — Associate
Most Selected
Tier Two

Member

Full membership. Residence access, a dedicated relationship manager, and the complete ecosystem of services.

  • All Associate benefits, fully extended
  • Residence access — up to 6 nights per month
  • Dedicated relationship manager (direct line)
  • Yacht charter — priority booking
  • Unlimited guest access
  • Executive boardroom access
  • Cryotherapy & IV therapy suites
  • Private wine cellar access
  • Curated travel desk
  • Polo & equestrian facility access
Apply — Member
Tier Three

Patron

The highest standing within Aso Dominion. Permanent residence access, a private floor, and every service at its fullest expression.

  • All Member benefits, fully extended
  • Permanent residence floor access
  • Priority booking — all services, all hours
  • Complimentary chauffeur (city limits)
  • Private chef on request — up to 14 guests
  • Longevity programme access
  • Exclusive Patron music & cultural evenings
  • Private jet coordination
  • Founding patron recognition
  • Committee nomination rights
Apply — Patron
