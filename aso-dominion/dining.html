Fine Dining

Aura
West African Haute Cuisine

The tower's flagship restaurant. Aura presents a 12-course tasting menu reinterpreting West African culinary heritage through a contemporary fine-dining lens. Only 28 covers. Bookings through your relationship manager.

Head Chef Adaeze Ike trained under three Michelin-starred kitchens before returning to Lagos with a singular intention: to place Nigerian flavour at the pinnacle of global fine dining.

Members Grill

Kobo
The All-Day Grill

Open from 7am to midnight, Kobo is the beating heart of the club. Breakfast meetings, working lunches, and long evenings over the grill. The menu changes with the markets. The standard never does.

Members may bring up to four guests per visit. Walk-in for breakfast and lunch; dinner reservations strongly recommended.

Obi
The Chef's Counter

Twelve seats. One counter. The chef decides the menu each morning based on what arrived from the markets and the fishing boats overnight. No substitutions. No printed menu. Trust is the only requirement.

Reservations open 30 days in advance for Members, 60 days for Patrons. One seating per evening, 7:30pm.

Rooftop Bar

Alto
Above It All

The highest members-only bar in Lagos. Open from 5pm, Alto offers an edited cocktail programme, a raw bar, and a view of the Atlantic horizon that makes every conversation feel consequential.

Dress code enforced. No laptops. This is an atmosphere, not a workspace.
