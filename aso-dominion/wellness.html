The Longevity Programme

Twelve weeks.
A permanent shift.

01

Weeks 1 – 2

Assessment & Baseline

Full biomarker panel, metabolic assessment, body composition analysis, sleep study, and intake consultation with the lead physician and nutritionist.

02

Weeks 3 – 6

Protocol Implementation

Personalised nutrition plan activated, training protocol begins, supplementation stack initiated, and twice-weekly check-ins with the clinical team.

03

Weeks 7 – 10

Recalibration

Mid-programme biomarker retest. Protocol adjusted based on observed response. Advanced modalities introduced — ozone therapy, peptide therapy (where indicated).

04

Weeks 11 – 12

Review & Blueprint

Final biomarker panel, full programme debrief, and delivery of a personalised long-term health blueprint. Ongoing quarterly reviews available post-programme.
